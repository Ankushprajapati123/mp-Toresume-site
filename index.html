Madhya Pradesh, known as the Heart Of India is a popular tourist destination with rich culture, history, and abundant wildlife. It boasts UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Khajuraho and Sanchi, stunning temples like Mahakaleshwar in Ujjain, and numerous national parks like Kanha and Bandhavgarh, making it a haven for wildlife enthusiasts.

Bhedaghat, located in Madhya Pradesh, is a town renowned for its stunning marble rocks and the Dhuandhar Falls on the Narmada River.

Destination: The nearest railway station is Bhedaghat Railway Station. Jabalpur is just 28 km away and accessible by auto or cab.

History: Once part of the ancient Tripuri Kingdom under the Kalchuri dynasty, Bhedaghat has long been revered for its spiritual and natural significance.

Panna, located in Madhya Pradesh, is famous for both its diamond mines and spiritual heritage.

It is the only region in India with active diamond mining, managed by the National Mineral Development Corporation (NMDC).

The Banke Bihari Temple, where Mahamati Prannath preached, makes it a key pilgrimage destination.
