Madhya Pradesh, known as the Heart Of India is a popular tourist destination with rich culture, history and wildlife. It includes UNESCO sites like Khajuraho and Sanchi with famous temples and beautiful national parks.

Explore the wonders of Madhya Pradesh — a timeless land of spirituality, nature and historic charm.

Bhedaghat

Bhedaghat is famous for marble rocks and the beautiful Dhuandhar waterfall on the Narmada river.

Destination: Nearest railway station is Bhedaghat. Jabalpur is only 28km away.

History: Part of ancient Tripuri kingdom under Kalchuri dynasty.

Panna

Panna is famous for diamond mines and its religious heritage.

It is the only place in India where diamonds are mined commercially by NMDC.

Banke Bihari Temple is an important pilgrimage site.
